EU Support in data economy:

The European Union actively supports small to midsize data center projects, which foster local tech independence and improve critical infrastructure.

European Data Strategy

The European Data Strategy intends to make the EU a leader in a data-driven society by creating a single market, allowing data to flow freely across the EU, benefiting businesses, researchers, and public administrations.

With the European Data Act, the strategy aims to make more data available, addressing legal, economic, and technical issues leading to data underutilization, and is expected to create €270 billion of additional GDP for EU Member States by 2028.

Investing in IoT

The European Unions Digital Decade policy program targets digital transformation with Cloud, edge, and IoT investments deemed necessary for European industries, particularly SMEs, to enhance technological sovereignty and support the data economy.

The EU funds projects to strengthen the European supply and value chains in the cloud-to-edge-to-IoT continuum, integrating computing, connectivity, IoT, AI, and cybersecurity.
